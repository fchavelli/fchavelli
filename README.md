I am a PhD student at [Inria](https://www.inria.fr/), and a member of the [VALDA project-team](https://team.inria.fr/valda/), a joint team between [Inria Paris](https://www.inria.fr/en/centre-inria-de-paris), [École Normale Supérieure](https://www.ens.psl.eu/), and [CNRS](https://www.cnrs.fr/).

My research focuses on machine learning, graphs, and time series. I am supervised by [Paul Boniol](https://boniolp.github.io/) and [Michael Thomazo](https://www.di.ens.fr/michael.thomazo/).

## Background

Previously, I completed a double M.Sc. program in:

- Computer Science at the [National University of Singapore](https://nus.edu.sg/)
- Applied Mathematics at [ENSTA Paris](https://www.ensta-paris.fr/), [Institut Polytechnique de Paris](https://www.ip-paris.fr/)

I also earned a B.Sc. in Applied Mathematics at [Université de Toulouse](https://www.univ-toulouse.fr/).
